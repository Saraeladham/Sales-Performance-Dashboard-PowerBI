# Sales Performance & Customer Insights Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis dashboard built using Power BI.  
It focuses on understanding business performance, customer behavior, and logistics efficiency.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit trends  
- Identify top-performing product categories  
- Understand customer segmentation  
- Evaluate shipping performance and delivery efficiency  

---

## Dashboard Features

### Business Performance Overview
- Total Sales: 2M  
- Total Profit: 283K  
- Sales and Profit trends over time  

### Customer & Product Insights
- Sales distribution by customer segment  
- Product category contribution  

### Sales Execution & Logistics
- Sales vs Target comparison  
- Shipping mode performance  
- Average delivery time analysis  

---

## Key Insights
- The **Consumer segment** contributes the highest share of total sales  
- **Office Supplies** is the top-performing product category  
- Sales show a consistent upward trend from 2011 to 2014  
- Shipping time varies significantly across different shipping modes  
- Profit growth is slower than sales growth, indicating potential cost inefficiencies
